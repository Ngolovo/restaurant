About Us

Our History

Homeland Restaurant was founded in 2010 in the heart of Food City, born from a passion for sharing East African culinary traditions with a global flair. Inspired by the vibrant flavors of Kenya, Tanzania, and Nigeria, our founders set out to create a welcoming space where fresh, locally sourced ingredients transform into dishes that tell a story of heritage and community. From humble beginnings, we’ve grown into a beloved dining destination, known for our signature Mandazi, Bhajias, and Moi Moi, each crafted with care to honor authentic recipes. Over the years, our commitment to quality and hospitality has earned us a loyal following, making Homeland a place where families, friends, and food lovers gather to celebrate. Today, we continue to innovate while staying true to our roots, offering a menu that blends tradition with creativity, ensuring every visit feels like coming home.

Our Mission

To provide an unforgettable dining experience with exceptional food and warm hospitality.

Our Team

At Homeland Restaurant, our team is the heartbeat of our culinary vision, bringing East African flavors to life with creativity and care. Led by Head Chef Amina Kwamboka, a master of Kenyan and Tanzanian cuisine, our chefs craft dishes like Mandazi and Moi Moi with authentic recipes and fresh, local ingredients. Our Sous Chef, Juma Omondi, ensures every plate is a work of art, blending tradition with innovation. The front-of-house team, guided by Hospitality Manager Sarah Wanjiku, creates a warm, welcoming atmosphere, making every guest feel at home. From our skilled bartenders mixing refreshing mocktails to our dedicated servers, each member is committed to exceptional service. Together, we strive to make every visit memorable, celebrating the vibrant spirit of East Africa. Join us at Homeland, where our team’s passion transforms dining into a heartfelt experience.
